Privacy Policy

TicketyPom is developed by Tom Nowell. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your information.

1. Information TicketyPom Collects

TicketyPom does not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal data to the developer. The app has no analytics, no tracking, and no external servers.

2. Access to Device Data

TicketyPom may request access to certain data or system features on your device (such as Reminders) in order to function.

  • stays on your device, and
  • is never sent to the developer.

Users can revoke these permissions at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security.

3. Integration With External Services

TicketyPom may integrate with external services to provide optional functionality.

  • Currently, the only integrated service is Apple Reminders, which is handled entirely by iOS.
  • Reminder data accessed by TicketyPom does not leave your device.
  • Apple may collect or process information as described in their own privacy policy. TicketyPom has no control over Apple's data practices.

If TicketyPom adds support for additional services in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Local App Data

All app-generated data—such as timer settings, preferences, or task metadata—remains stored locally on your device only.

5. Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

TicketyPom does not use third-party analytics tools or advertising SDKs.

6. Children's Privacy

TicketyPom does not collect any personal data from users of any age.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when new features or integrations are added. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, you can reach the developer at: ticketypom.com/support.html
