# Support

We're here to help! Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get in touch with our support team.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I use the Pomodoro timer?
The Pomodoro timer is built into the app. Simply start a session, focus on your task, and take breaks as prompted.

### Can I sync my tasks between devices?
Yes! TicketyPom supports syncing across iOS and watchOS devices.

### How do I contact support?
You can reach us at [support@ticktypom.com](mailto:support@ticktypom.com).

## Contact Us
If you need further assistance, feel free to email us at [support@ticktypom.com](mailto:support@ticktypom.com).
