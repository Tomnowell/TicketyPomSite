# Welcome to TicketyPom

TicketyPom is your ultimate productivity companion, combining the simplicity of a task list with the effectiveness of the Pomodoro technique. Whether you're managing your daily tasks or focusing on deep work, TicketyPom is here to help.

## Why Choose TicketyPom?
- **Boost Productivity**: Stay focused and accomplish more with structured work sessions.
- **Stay Organized**: Manage your tasks effortlessly with a clean and intuitive interface.
- **On-the-Go Access**: Available on iOS and watchOS, so you can stay productive anywhere.

- **Task List**: Organize your tasks with ease.
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Work in focused intervals with regular breaks.
- **Sync Across Devices**: Keep your tasks and progress updated on all your devices.
